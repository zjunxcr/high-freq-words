Day 8 · 第71-80词 · 2026-04-29
📝 今日单词
#71
best /best/
Lv.1 adj./adv. 最好的；最佳
例句 Example
She always tries her best in everything she does.
她在做任何事情时都尽力而为。
#72
between /bɪˈtwiːn/
Lv.1 prep. 在……之间
例句 Example
The school is located between the park and the library.
学校位于公园和图书馆之间。
#73
big /bɪɡ/
Lv.1 adj. 大的；重要的
例句 Example
They made a big decision to move to a new city.
他们做出了搬到新城市的重大决定。
#74
bill /bɪl/
Lv.1 n. 账单；钞票；法案
例句 Example
The waiter brought the bill at the end of the meal.
服务员在用餐结束时送来了账单。
#75
bird /bɜːrd/
Lv.1 n. 鸟；人；飞机
例句 Example
A small bird was singing in the tree outside.
一只小鸟在外面的树上唱歌。
#76
blank /blæŋk/
Lv.1 adj./n. 空白的；空白
例句 Example
She stared at the blank page, not knowing what to write.
她盯着空白的页面，不知道该写什么。
#77
blood /blʌd/
Lv.1 n. 血液；血统；血气
例句 Example
The doctor said her blood pressure was a bit high.
医生说她的血压有点高。
#78
blow /bləʊ/
Lv.1 v. 吹；吹气；爆发
例句 Example
A strong wind started to blow when we were outside.
我们在外面的时候，一阵强风刮了起来。
#79
boat /bəʊt/
Lv.1 n. 小船；轮船；艇形物
例句 Example
We rented a small boat and went out on the lake.
我们租了一条小船，在湖上划行。
#80
body /ˈbɒdi/
Lv.1 n. 身体；团体；主体
例句 Example
Regular exercise is good for both the body and mind.
有规律的锻炼对身心都有好处。
