Day 1 · 第1-10词 · 2026-04-21
📝 今日单词
#1
a /ə/ /eɪ/
Lv.1 art. 一个；任一
例句 Example
I have a book.
我有一本书。
#2
about /əˈbaʊt/
Lv.1 prep. 关于；大约 adv. 大约
例句 Example
Tell me about your day.
告诉我你今天过得怎么样。
#3
all /ɔːl/
Lv.1 adj. 所有的；全部的 pron. 全部
例句 Example
All my friends are here.
我所有的朋友都在这儿。
#4
also /ˈɔːlsəʊ/
Lv.1 adv. 也；同样；而且
例句 Example
She also likes music.
她也喜欢音乐。
#5
am /æm/ /əm/
Lv.1 v. 是（I的be动词形式）
例句 Example
I am a student.
我是一个学生。
#6
an /ən/ /æn/
Lv.1 art. 一个（用于元音前）
例句 Example
This is an apple.
这是一个苹果。
#7
and /ænd/ /ənd/
Lv.1 conj. 和；又；而
例句 Example
I like tea and coffee.
我喜欢茶和咖啡。
#8
are /ɑːr/ /ər/
Lv.1 v. 是（you/we/they的be动词形式）
例句 Example
They are my classmates.
他们是我的同学。
#9
as /æz/ /əz/
Lv.1 prep. 作为；如同 conj. 当…时
例句 Example
He works as a teacher.
他当老师工作。
#10
at /æt/
Lv.1 prep. 在；以；向；朝
例句 Example
See you at school.
学校见。

📺 Scene #2 · "about"

📺 Scene #3 · "all"

Phoebe: I invited **all** my friends! Ross: All of them? That's fifty people! Phoebe: Yeah! The more, the merrier!
菲比：我邀请了我所有的朋友！ 罗斯：所有人？那大概有五十个人！ 菲比：对啊！人越多越热闹嘛！

📺 Scene #4 · "also"

Chandler: Monica is cooking dinner tonight. Joey: Nice! What's she making? Chandler: Pasta. She's **also** making garlic bread. Joey: I love that woman!
钱德勒：莫妮卡今晚做饭。 乔伊：太好了！她做什么？ 钱德勒：意面。她还做了蒜香面包。 乔伊：我爱死她了！

📺 Scene #5 · "am"

Joey: What time is it? Monica: It's eight. Why? Joey: I **am** late for my audition! Rachel: Good luck, Joey!
乔伊：几点了？ 莫妮卡：八点了。怎么了？ 乔伊：我试镜要迟到了！ 瑞秋：祝你好运，乔伊！

📺 Scene #6 · "an"

Ross: I have **an** idea! Rachel: Every time you say that... Ross: This time it's **an** excellent plan! Rachel: We'll see about that.
罗斯：我有个主意！ 瑞秋：每次你这么说…… 罗斯：这次是个极好的计划！ 瑞秋：那就走着瞧吧。

📺 Scene #7 · "and"

Monica: Did you buy the milk **and** eggs? Chandler: I got the milk... Monica: **And** the eggs? Chandler: ...I forgot the eggs.
莫妮卡：你买了牛奶和鸡蛋吗？ 钱德勒：牛奶买了…… 莫妮卡：那鸡蛋呢？ 钱德勒：……我忘了买鸡蛋。

📺 Scene #8 · "are"

Phoebe: Where **are** we going tonight? Rachel: We **are** going to that new restaurant. Joey: **are** you sure it's open? Monica: They **are** open until eleven.
菲比：我们今晚去哪儿啊？ 瑞秋：我们去那家新餐厅。 乔伊：确定开着门吗？ 莫妮卡：营业到十一点。

📺 Scene #9 · "as"

Rachel: You're acting **as** if nothing happened! Ross: Well, what do you want me to say? Rachel: Don't act **as** cool **as** usual! Ross: It's not **as** bad **as** you think.
瑞秋：你表现得好像什么都没发生一样！ 罗斯：那你希望我说什么？ 瑞秋：别跟平时那么淡定！ 罗斯：没你想得那么严重。

📺 Scene #10 · "at"

Chandler: Look **at** this! My boss wants me to work Saturday! Monica: Again? You were **at** the office all week! Chandler: He said be **at** work at nine. What can I do?
钱德勒：看看这个！老板要我周六加班！ 莫妮卡：又来？你这周都在公司待着了！ 钱德勒：他说九点到。我能怎么办？
